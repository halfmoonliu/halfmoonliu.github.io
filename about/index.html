LIU Yun-Chung

Machine Learning Research Engineer

As an enthusiast of data, I always seek to utilize data to inform people of information hidden and to improve people’s well-being. I am a hungry learner, always looking for new algorithms (e.g. machine learning (ML), deep learning), applying them to solve real-world problems, and trying to enhance the performance of given tasks. For some of my recent projects, please have a look at the posts here or go to my GitHub Page.

I am an experienced data analyst with hand-on experience building ML applications. With my solid understanding in ML and its math foundations, I look to enhance current solutions with data and deploy real-world solutions. Currently, I am working at National Taiwan University Hospital, developing proofs-of-concepts of ML-based medical decision support systems on various application scenarios (e.g. prognosis prediction, risk evaluation, diagnosis, and intervention recommendation). In addition, I support research data analysis requests to help physicians identify important clinical manifestations and biomarkers in different diseases and cohorts. Before this, I have worked as a sales data analyst at E-Sun bank and a human resources data analyst at Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC).
